## Type

## Goal
Ensure SOP language is safe for internal audit, HR, and client review.

## Description
Apply the following non-substantive clarifications:

- Add language stating SOPs provide **operational guidance** and do not replace:
  - Professional judgment
  - Legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations
- Clarify that:
  - Recruiters remain accountable for final decisions
  - AI and automation inform but do not decide
- Reinforce that:
  - Judgment logic and confidence calibration remain internal
  - Only approved summaries and outcomes may be shared externally

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] No change to behavioral intent
- [ ] No new process requirements introduced
- [ ] Language reviewed for neutrality and clarity
- [ ] Safe for enterprise distribution
