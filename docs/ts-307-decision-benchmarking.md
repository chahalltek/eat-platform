# TS-307 – Decision Benchmarking (Internal)

- **Tier:** Tier 7 — Strategic / Optional (Do Not Rush)
- **Type:** Analytics
- **Priority:** P4
- **Epic:** Insights

## Goal

Understand where judgment is strong or breaking down.

## Description

Compare decision patterns across key dimensions to surface where teams are thriving or struggling:

- Teams
- Regions
- Role families

## Constraints

- Diagnostic only
- No ranking or gamification

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Aggregated, anonymized views
- [ ] Trend-focused metrics
- [ ] Clear non-punitive framing
